Metric Conversion

Shipping charges will be based on the dimensional weight of the shipment when the dimensional weight exceeds the actual weight.
Dimensional weight is determined by using M 5 Continent Logistics Solution (P) Ltd. volumetric standard.
Step
Step 1:Calculate cubic size :
Multiply the length by the width and by the height (each rounded up to the nearest whole centimetre) of each package Step 2:Determine the dimensional weight :
Divide the cubic size of the package by 5000 to determine the dimensional weight in kilograms.
Note:
The maximum weight of a package may vary by destination.
